Summer Festival Highlights

April 20, 2024 • Events

Our annual summer dance festival was filled with incredible performances, workshops, and memories. Dancers of all ages and levels came together to celebrate their passion for movement.

The event featured group choreographies, solo showcases, and surprise guest performances. From energetic hip-hop sets to emotional contemporary pieces, the stage was alive with creativity.

We are grateful to everyone who participated, volunteered, and cheered from the audience. Your energy and support make this community so special.

Missed the festival this year? Don’t worry – we’re already planning the next one. Keep an eye on our events page for upcoming dates and opportunities to get involved.
