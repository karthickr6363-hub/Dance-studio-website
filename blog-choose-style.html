How to Choose the Right Dance Style for You

May 25, 2024 • Dance Tips

With so many dance styles to choose from, it can be hard to know where to start. The key is to consider your personality, goals, and the kind of music that moves you the most.

If you love high energy and bold expression, styles like hip-hop, jazz, or Bollywood might be a great fit. If you prefer elegance and control, consider ballet or contemporary. Latin styles like salsa and bachata are perfect if you enjoy partner work and social dancing.

Think about whether you're dancing for fitness, performance, creativity, or fun. At JustDance, our instructors are happy to guide you through trial classes so you can discover what feels best in your body.

Remember: there is no “right” or “wrong” choice – the best style is the one that keeps you excited to come back to the studio.
