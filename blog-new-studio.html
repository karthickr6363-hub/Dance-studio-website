New Studio Facilities Opening Soon

May 12, 2024 • Studio Updates

We are excited to announce that our brand-new studio facilities are opening soon! The expansion will allow us to welcome more students while offering an even better learning environment.

The new space will feature larger practice rooms, upgraded sound systems, improved flooring, and enhanced lighting designed specifically for dance training and performance.

These improvements will give our dancers more room to move, better acoustics for choreography, and a comfortable space to warm up and cool down before and after class.

Stay tuned for our official opening date and special launch workshops – we can’t wait to dance with you in our upgraded home.
